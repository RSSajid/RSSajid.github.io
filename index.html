About


Zarmeenah is a self taught Creative/ Bridal Hair and Makeup Artist, based in the Midlands, UK. She has undergone hair training with Asiana bridal and is always striving to keep up with the latest trends in the beauty industry. Having studied Art and Design, she has a keen eye for detail and is always coming up with innovative ideas and looks.

"I love what I do, making women look and feel beautiful on their special day! I ensure I listen to my clients' requirements as well as give my own input and advice; to make sure my clients are satisfied with the outcome. Professionalism is vital to me, hence I try my level best to cater my clients every need. Have the passion and the impossible will happen."
